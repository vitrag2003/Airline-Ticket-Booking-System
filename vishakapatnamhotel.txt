1-Shree Lakshmi Guest House
Featuring free WiFi and air conditioning, Shree Lakshmi Guest House is located in Visakhapatnam, 13 km from Indira Gandhi Zoological Park. Daba Gardens is 8 km away. 
Free private parking is available on site.
All units have a satellite flat-screen TV. There is a private bathroom with a shower in each unit. Towels are provided.
Rating: 7.1/10

Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.
Dondaparithy is 17 km from Treebo Trend M V Boutique, while Diamond Park is 18 km away. The nearest airport is Visakhapatnam Airport, 24 km from the hotel.
